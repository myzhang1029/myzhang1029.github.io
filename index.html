About Me

I am currently working towards my B.S. Engineering Physics degree at University of California, San Diego.

To keep myself from becoming bored, I work on random programming or physical projects like IoT light switches, audio amplifiers, and amateur radio.

Other people usually describe me as “very handy,” and I treat other people’s problems as my own. I try to be altruistic and do whatever I can for those around me.

I “experiment” with random things in my free time, like cooking, writing, or music. I like to experiment with the guitar, the harmonica and the accordion.

As an environmentalist, I love to go outdoors, collect rubbish, and leave no trace.
