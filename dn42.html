DN42 Network

I am AS4242420893 on the dn42 network, operating two prefixes as MAIYUN-DN42:
172.23.6.160/27
fdc0:d227:306::/48

Currently, there are two permanent nodes, physically located in California (AS7377) and Toronto (AS31898). Internal connections are tunnelled via ZeroTier. Internal routing is done with a custom version of Bird running iBGP. Experimentation with OSPF and Babel is underway.

Peering

Please contact me at dn42admin at maiyun.me with your details. Everything is casual, and there are no clear-cut peering requirements. I prefer using WireGuard and speaking BGP with multiprotocol and extended next hop support.

My internal network is capable of routing 44Net traffic as well. I'll be happy to exchange those prefixes if you can show proof of allocation.
